About Me

Kenan James Bali

I am the third son of a loving American Mother and a loving Turkish Father. Born 1989 Turkey/Ankara.I was lucky enough to leave my country at age 16 to come to the land of opportunity. I have been working and moving through careers .I've worked at an airport(fuel technician) , apartment complex (maintenance) , retail (sam's club) , pet store (aquarium maintenance) , and made my final decision to get some education under my belt.




My Epic Dog

Well here is my bud "Rascal" . Found his Mother wondering around the airport i used to work at .She didn't get no choice but we took her in. We wanted to keep it original so called her Lady hah . She ended up having 12 puppies and Rascal here was the runt of the litter , but the main reason we couldn't let him go was that he would howl everytime he hears a harmonica playing .Awesome right !! , yeah sold his brothers and sisters tho .That's too many dogs... My mom probably wouldn't agree.
